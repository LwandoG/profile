About me

I am a well-reasoned, aspiring software developer with a keen interest in everything technology. I enjoy abstract, logical thought to bring solutions to real-life problems. I believe all problems can be solved if you divide them into smaller manageable problems, what we call “divide and conquer” in programming terms. I was first exposed to programming in high school. I took Information Technology as a subject in grade 10 where I was exposed to the programming thought process through a program called Scratch. In the following grades, I got to delve into the Java programming language. I developed my first mobile app for the BlackBerry Mobile Application Development challenge hosted at UWC in 2013. Since then I always knew it’s what I wanted to do for the rest of my life.
